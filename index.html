About REB/PAC


REB/PAC was started by David Schaefer in January, 1983 with the mission to provide products and printing to companies that valued quality, professionalism and understood that terms are important. Even the name of the company was derived from a combination of my lovely wife's name REBecca and the PAC, well, let's just say it's an acronym for Please Accelerate Check. ;-)
