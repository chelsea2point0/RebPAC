Envelopes


  • A-Style

    • Otherwise referred to as announcement envelopes, these envelopes are commonly used for invitations, promotional mailings, photographs, brochures, and (of course) announcements.

  • Baronial

    • Baronial envelopes have a pointed flap and are ideal for formal mailings such as wedding, birthday, and anniversary invitations.

  • Booklet

    • The flap on the long side of the envelope is the defining feature of the booklet envelope. This design makes it easy to enclose a brochure, catalog, or reports that you don't want folded.

  • Catalog

    • Open-end catalog envelopes are similar to booklet envelopes, but with the flap on the short side. These are commonly used for magazines, calendars, photos, and reports.

  • Commercial

    • One of REB/PAC's most popular envelopes. Ideal for billing, notices, and other business correspondence.

  • Square

    • As the name suggests, these envelopes are square in shape and often used for advertising, announcements, and specialty greeting cards.

  • Additional sizes and varieties

    • Also available: Airmail, Clasp, Coins, Expansion, Inter-Office, Jumbo, Kost Kut $ 2-Way Remittance, Translucent, Tyvek, and Windows
