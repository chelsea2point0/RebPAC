About REB/PAC

REB/PAC was founded in January, 1983 by David Schaefer with the mission to provide products and printing to companies that valued quality, professionalism and understood that terms are important. Even the name of the company was derived from a combination of David's lovely wife's name 'REBecca' and the PAC, well, let's just say it's an acronym for 'Please Accelerate Check'. ;-)
